SKI FACTS

! SkiFacts provides a combination of mapping and data charts to help skiers and snowboarders choose a European ski resort

! Piste and resort data on 130 of Europe's most popular ski reosrts

! Filter and sort your data to home in on the best resort for your needs

! Compare piste statistics on dozens of resorts side by side

! Discover which resorts are at the highest altitude, or which has the greatest vertical descent

! SkiFacts is a mapping and data visualisation project by Peter Elvidge built using Bootstrap, D3 javascript library, and CartoDB
